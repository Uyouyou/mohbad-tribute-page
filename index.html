THE ONE

IIerioluwa Oladimeji Aloba, born on 8 June 1996 in Ketu, Lagos State, Nigeria, professionally known as Mohbad, was a rapper, singer, and songwriter. He was formerly signed to Naira Marley's Marlian Records and left the label in 2022. He released his debut album, Light, in the fourth quarter of 2020 as a follow up to his viral song Ponmo, which featured Naira Marley and Lil Kesh.

A STELLAR ICON

Mohbad was nominated five times for The Beatz Awards 2021. He was also listed in Audiomack's top 21 Afrobeat artists of 2021. In 2022, he released Peace, produced by Rexxie. He peaked on TurnTable charts top 50 in 2021 and 100 in 2022. Peace record peaked number one Apple Music Chart Nigeria. He died on the 12th of September 2023.
